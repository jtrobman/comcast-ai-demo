Comcast Business public promise language says that if a customer reports a service interruption that was Comcast's issue, the customer will receive a bill credit, and that this does not apply to scheduled maintenance. Comcast Business also describes 24/7 customer service support from experts who understand business needs.

Comcast Business billing guidance says credits can appear on a bill for several reasons and gives a lengthy service outage as an example where Comcast Business may apply a credit to an account. The same billing page directs customers to sign in to My Account for live, personal support, or call customer support for help.

Demo use: This source supports cautious credit-related language: acknowledge the request, document the impact, verify outage/interruption details, and avoid promising money back until an eligibility tool or approved policy confirms the customer qualifies.
