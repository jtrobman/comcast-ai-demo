Comcast Business My Account gives customers access to Comcast Business services from anywhere. Public Comcast Business material describes My Account as a place to manage product features such as WiFi networks and Call Forwarding, pay bills, enroll in Paperless Billing and Auto Pay, check connection status, restart a modem, manage WiFi settings, and find Xfinity hotspots available with Comcast Business Internet.

The Comcast Business App is described as a mobile way to manage account details and settings, troubleshoot technical issues, make bill payments, sign up for Auto Pay, customize WiFi settings for Business Internet, view TV channel lineups, and manage Business Voice features.

Demo use: This source supports guided support language that sends customers to authenticated Comcast Business self-service surfaces for connection checks, modem restart, WiFi settings, and app-based troubleshooting. It does not prove live account state or outage status; those must come from operational tools.
