Comcast Business public guidance for weather-related outages says customers can sign in to My Account or the Comcast Business App for real-time service updates and outage text alerts. For Internet issues when the business still has electricity, customers can access the Comcast Business Internet Support Tool from My Account to troubleshoot and restart a modem from desktop or mobile.

If a customer experiences loss of service and local power is also out, Comcast Business says the local utility provider must restore power before Comcast technicians can start restoring services. Once power has been restored, customers are advised to restart the modem to reestablish the connection.

Related Comcast Business construction guidance says planned network construction can interrupt Comcast Business and Xfinity services. During construction, Internet-connected devices and services may be offline, and customers can register a mobile number in My Account for text alerts. When service returns, Comcast Business recommends restarting the gateway with the Comcast Business App, My Account, or manually. Customers can visit the Service Status Center to see whether service has been restored and should contact Comcast Business Support if issues continue.
